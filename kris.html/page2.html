San Francisco High School

Quezon City, Philippines

About Us

San Francisco High School is a public secondary school located in Bago Bantay, Quezon City, Philippines. Founded in 1963, we are part of the Quezon City Division of the Department of Education.

Our Curriculum

We offer a junior and senior high school curriculum, including academic tracks and technical-vocational-livelihood (TVL) tracks. We focus on student leadership, sports, and various extracurricular activities.
