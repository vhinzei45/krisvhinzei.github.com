Welcome to San Francisco High School

About Us

San Francisco High School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing a transformative learning experience for students. With a rich history and a commitment to excellence, we strive to empower our students to become leaders and lifelong learners.

Academics

Our rigorous academic program offers a wide range of courses, allowing students to explore various disciplines and discover their passions. With highly qualified teachers and state-of-the-art facilities, we provide an environment that fosters intellectual growth and personal development.

Extracurricular Activities

At San Francisco High School, we believe in the importance of holistic education. We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, clubs, and community service opportunities. These activities enable students to explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and foster a sense of community.

Admissions

We welcome students from all backgrounds who are eager to learn and contribute to our vibrant school community. To apply to San Francisco High School, please visit our admissions page for more information.
